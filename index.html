This is a new service. If you need any help please contact us. This is a trial service for family and friends of prisoners at HMP Brixton, HMP Bullingdon and HMP High Down only.

Sending money to a prisoner

Have your say about sending money through the post in this quick survey.

You can send money to a prisoner by making a bank transfer. You can do this with online, mobile or telephone banking, but not in person at your bank branch.

You can only use this service if you’re sending money to prisoners in HMP Brixton, HMP Bullingdon or HMP High Down.

You'll need:

  • a payment reference to identify the prisoner
  • the prison service bank account and sort code

Use this tool to check the prisoner details you have are correct, and to generate a valid payment reference.

Once you have a valid payment reference you will be told the prison service bank account and sort code.

Before you start

The payment reference uses:

  • Prisoner number
  • Prisoner date of birth

If you don’t know the prisoner number or date of birth, the prisoner will be able to tell you what they are.
